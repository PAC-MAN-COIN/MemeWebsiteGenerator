Volume Bot Service

Increase your memecoin's trading volume automatically to attract attention.

What is a Volume Bot?

In the fast-paced world of memecoins, visibility is key. Our Volume Bot service helps simulate trading activity for your token on Solana. By funding the bot with SOL, it will automatically perform buy transactions for your token on decentralized exchanges (like Raydium), increasing the reported trading volume.

Higher volume can make your project appear more active and potentially attract more organic traders and investors.

Pricing & Volume Generated

Fund the bot with SOL to generate approximate USD volume. The minimum deposit is 4 SOL.

Note: Generated volume is an estimate and depends on market conditions and bot configuration. The SOL deposited is used to cover transaction fees and the cost of buying the token.

Bot Speed & Duration Tiers

The "speed" refers to the approximate interval between the bot's buy transactions. The duration indicates roughly how long the bot will run for a standard deposit at that speed (longer intervals mean the funds last longer).

  • 1 second interval (~15m - 30m duration)
  • 5 seconds interval (~1h duration)
  • 15 seconds interval (~2h - 3h duration)
  • 1 minute interval (~4h - 5h duration)
  • 2 minutes interval (~5h - 6h duration)
  • 5 minutes interval (~6h+ duration)
  • 10 minutes interval (~10h+ duration)
  • 30 minutes interval (~1 Day+ duration)
  • 1 hour interval (~4 Days+ duration)

Speed selection and specific run times can be discussed when setting up the service.
