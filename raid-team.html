What is a Raid Team?

A Raid Team is a dedicated group focused on promoting your coin by strategically engaging with posts from crypto influencers and relevant accounts on X (Twitter). This involves activities like replying, liking, and retweeting to increase your project's visibility within the target community.

We utilize tools like Telegram bots (e.g., RaidBot) to organize these efforts efficiently, track engagement, earn "energy" for participation, and potentially climb leaderboards for added exposure. Classic raids to involve the community without specifically targeting the raidboard are part of our standard service.

How It Works

Upon starting a service package, we create a dedicated, private Telegram group for seamless communication. This group includes your team representatives and our assigned Raid Leader.

Coordination Process:

  • Raid targets and specific post "recipes" (content suggestions) are shared within the group.
  • You can directly interact with the Raid Leader to coordinate specific actions, adjust strategy, or provide key project updates.
  • Engagement progress and feedback are regularly discussed.
